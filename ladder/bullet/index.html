Lichess Bullet Ladder - August 2021
In the ladder below, players are assigned ratings which may better reflect mutual strengths over a long period of time than the day-to-day ratings or the highest ratings. To achieve this, first all the top bullet players with high maximum ratings were extracted from the games database. Only those players were selected who either (a) have a maximum bullet rating of 3000+, or (b) have a maximum bullet rating of 2900+ and the IM/WGM title, or (c) have a maximum bullet rating of 2800+ and the GM title. From these, all closed and TOS-marked accounts and accounts with few or no games against other top players were filtered out.

Then, for this list of 346 players, lifetime mutual bullet scores were extracted from the database as well. For instance, DrNykterstein and alireza2003 currently have a lifetime bullet score of 437.0-332.0. Ratings were then assigned to all players in an iterative process such that the overall scores match the expected scores with these relative ratings. The final rating list was then normalized to have an equal average as the initial list of highest bullet ratings.
