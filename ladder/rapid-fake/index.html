Lichess Rapid Ladder - August 2021
In the ladder below, players are assigned ratings which may better reflect mutual strengths over a long period of time than the day-to-day ratings or the highest ratings. To achieve this, first all the top rapid players with high maximum ratings were extracted from the games database. Only those players were selected who either (a) have a maximum rapid rating of 2600+, or (b) have a maximum rapid rating of 2500+ and the IM/WGM title, or (c) have a maximum rapid rating of 2500+ and the GM title. From these, we filtered out all closed and TOS-marked accounts, as well as accounts with few or no games against other top players, to obtain the following list of players.

Then, for this list of 446 players, lifetime mutual rapid scores between these top players were extracted from the database. Ratings were then assigned to all players in an iterative process such that the overall scores in all these games match the expected scores with these assigned, relative ratings. The final rating list was normalized to have an equal average as the initial list of highest rapid ratings.
