Lichess Blitz Ladder - August 2021
In the ladder below, players are assigned ratings which may better reflect mutual strengths over a long period of time than the day-to-day ratings or the highest ratings. To achieve this, first all the top blitz players with high maximum ratings were extracted from the games database. Only those players were selected who either (a) have a maximum blitz rating of 2900+, or (b) have a maximum blitz rating of 2800+ and the IM/WGM title, or (c) have a maximum blitz rating of 2700+ and the GM title. From these, we filtered out all closed and TOS-marked accounts, as well as accounts with few or no games against other top players, to obtain the following list of players.

Then, for this list of 400 players, lifetime mutual blitz scores were extracted from the database as well. For instance, DrNykterstein and penguingim1 currently have a lifetime blitz score of 18.0-3.0. Ratings were then assigned to all players in an iterative process such that the overall scores match the expected scores with these relative ratings. The final rating list was normalized to have an equal average as the initial list of highest blitz ratings.
